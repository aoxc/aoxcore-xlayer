# Contributing to AOXCAN Sovereign Ecosystem

## 🏛️ The Sovereign Mandate
AOXCAN is not just a repository; it is a **Sovereign Autonomous Network**. We operate under a strict **7-DAO (Sentinel) Structure**. Every contribution, whether it is a line of code or a strategic proposal, must align with our core principles: **Honesty, Justice, and Neural Integrity.**

## 🛡️ The AI-Overseer Audit
All Pull Requests (PRs) and contributions are subject to a mandatory **AI-Overseer Audit** powered by the **OKB-001 Guardian Model**. 
- **Code Quality:** Must meet the "Surgical" standards (V2.1.x).
- **Security Check:** Zero-tolerance for backdoors, unoptimized opcodes, or logic flaws.
- **Protocol Loyalty:** Contributions must strengthen the ecosystem's longevity, not personal gain.

## 🚀 How to Contribute

1. **Fork & Branch:** Create a feature branch from `develop`.
2. **Surgical Coding:** Follow the existing Go, Rust, and Solidity patterns. Keep the "silence of the opcodes" in mind.
3. **Submit PR:** Clearly define the impact on which Sentinel (e.g., Treasury, Security) is affected.
4. **Pass the Gauntlet:** Wait for the **Overseer** to perform the neural audit. If the AI flags a risk, the contribution will be rejected automatically.

## ⚖️ Governance & Rewards
Approved contributions may be recognized by the **Governance DAO** and potentially rewarded from the **AOXCAN Treasury**. We value long-term architects over short-term "hackers."

## 💬 Communication
For strategic discussions, join our official channels or reach out via the designated **Gateway Sentinel** protocols.
